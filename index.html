A finding aid for tea resources

Tea is an aromatic beverage prepared by pouring hot or boiling water over cured or fresh leaves of Camellia Sinensis, an evergreen shrub native to China and other East Asian countries. It can also refer to a drink composed of herbal or floral elements, like roobois or lavender, used by itself or combined with the Camellia Sinensis.
